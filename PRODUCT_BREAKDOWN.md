# KithGrid Product Breakdown

## Product Summary
**KithGrid** is a hyper-local community management platform designed to connect neighbors, enhance safety, and streamline HOA communications. It acts as a central digital hub where residents can organize events, share resources, trade items, and access critical community information in a cohesive, secure environment.

## Navigation Breakdown

### 1. Dashboard
The home landing page that gives residents an immediate "pulse" of the community.
*   **Snapshot**: Displays a personalized welcome message and the community name.
*   **Widgets**: Features a summary of the next 3 **Upcoming Events** and critical **Announcements** (e.g., pool maintenance, trash delays).

### 2. Forum
The town square for public discussion.
*   **Categorized Feeds**: Filter discussions by *General, Safety, Events, Lost & Found,* and *Recommendations*.
*   **Interaction**: Residents can post updates, comment on threads, and "like" useful information.
*   **Context**: Quick links to direct message authors directly from their posts.

### 3. Messages
A private communication channel for 1-on-1 interactions.
*   **Inbox**: Manages active conversations with unread message indicators.
*   **Chat**: Real-time messaging interface for coordinating with neighbors privately.

### 4. Neighbors
A searchable directory of all residents.
*   **Discovery**: Search neighbors by **Name**, **Skills** (e.g., "Nurse", "Plumber"), or **Equipment** they are willing to lend (e.g., "Ladder", "Power Drill").
*   **Invites**: A system to send email invitations with unique access codes to new residents.

### 5. Events
The community calendar for social and official gatherings.
*   **Management**: View details for HOA meetings, block parties, or social clubs.
*   **RSVP**: Residents can confirm their attendance directly on event cards.
*   **Creation**: Tools for residents to create and publish their own community events.

### 6. Marketplace
A local "Buy / Sell / Trade" platform exclusive to the neighborhood.
*   **Listings**: Users can post items with photos, prices, and descriptions. Includes tags for "Free" or "Negotiable".
*   **Lifecycle**: Listings automatically expire after 30 days to keep the feed fresh.
*   **Status**: Visual indicators for "Sold" or "Expired" items.

### 7. Service Pros
A database of trusted local professionals recommended by neighbors.
*   **Verified Reviews**: Listings for handymen, roofers, and landscapers that specifically highlight *which neighbor* recommended them.
*   **Ratings**: Star ratings and brief reviews to help vet vendors.

### 8. Local Guide
A curated list of nearby amenities.
*   **Places**: Recommendations for local restaurants, parks, and entertainment.
*   **Details**: Shows distance from the neighborhood, ratings, and category (Activity vs. Dining).

### 9. Community Resources
A distinct section for shared community assets.
*   **Reservables**: View and book shared facilities (like a Clubhouse) or community-owned tools.
*   **Availability**: Real-time status checking (e.g., "Available Now").

### 10. HOA Info
The administrative record for the community.
*   **Info Hub**: Displays Board Member contacts and Dues information/payment dates.
*   **Document Library**: A repository for PDFs (Bylaws, Financial Reports) and external links (City Zoning), complete with an inline previewer.

### 11. Emergency
A dedicated safety center.
*   **SOS Alert**: A prominently displayed button to instantly notify designated emergency contacts.
*   **Medical Neighbors**: Automatically filters the Neighbors directory to show only those with medical training (MD, RN, EMT, CPR) for urgent help.
*   **Quick Dial**: One-tap access to 911, Poison Control, and personal emergency contacts.

### 12. Settings
User account management for profile details, notification preferences, and emergency contact setup.

### 13. Admin Console *(Visible to Admins only)*
*   **User Management**: Tools to view, edit, and manage resident accounts and permissions.
