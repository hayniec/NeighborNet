## Getting Started

### Accessing the App
Simply navigate to the URL provided by your community administrator.

### Your Profile
Currently, your profile is auto-generated upon entry.
*   **Settings**: Go to the **Settings** page (Grid icon on sidebar -> Settings) to manage your view preferences.
*   **Role**: You are assigned a role of **Resident** or **Admin**. This determines which features you can access.

---

## Dashboard Overview
The **Dashboard** is your central hub.
*   **Announcements**: Important updates from the HOA board (e.g., "Pool Closed for Maintenance").
*   **Upcoming Events**: A quick look at the next 3 events on the calendar.
*   **Quick Stats**: See how many neighbors are online.

## Community Features

### Forum
A space for general discussion, questions, and polls.
*   **View**: Scroll through posts from neighbors.
*   **Interact**: Click on a post to read comments or add your own (Simulated in this version).
*   **Create**: Click specific category tabs (General, Safety, Lost & Found) to filter conversations.

### Neighbors Directory
Find and connect with people on your street.
*   **Search**: Use the global search (top right) to find neighbors by name or skill.
*   **Profile**: Click a neighbor card to view their:
    *   **Skills**: e.g., "Plumber", "Notary".
    *   **Shared Equipment**: Tools they are willing to lend.
    *   **Contact**: Send them a direct message.

### Events
Stay up to date with social mixers and official meetings.
*   **Calendar**: View all upcoming events sorted by date.
*   **RSVP**: Click "RSVP" to let the organizer know you are coming. You can specify the number of guests.

### Marketplace
Buy, sell, or give away items locally.
*   **Listings**: Browse items for sale. Expired items are automatically dimmed.
*   **Post Item**: Click the "+" button to list your own item. You can set a price or mark it as "Free".
*   **Expiration**: Listings differ from standard social postsâ€”they automatically expire after 30 days to keep the feed fresh.

### Community Resources
Reserve shared amenities like the **Clubhouse**, **Pool Pavilion**, or shared tools (**Pressure Washer**).
*   **Check Availability**: Icons indicate if an item is available "Now" or booked.
*   **Reserve**: Click "Reserve" on any item to open the booking calendar. Select your Date and Time window to submit a request.

### Service Pros
A curated list of trusted local vendors (Plumbers, Electricians, etc.) recommended by your neighbors.
*   **Vetting**: See which neighbor recommended the pro.
*   **Details**: View phone numbers and specialties at a glance.

### Documents
The official repository for HOA bylaws, forms, and meeting minutes.
*   **Download**: Click any file to download it (Simulated).
*   **Categories**: Filter by "Financials", "Rules", or "Forms".

## Communication

### Messages
Private, direct chats with your neighbors.
*   **Inbox**: View your recent conversations on the left.
*   **Chat**: Click a name to open the chat window.
*   **Send**: Type your message and hit Enter to send instantly.

---

## Admin Console
*Access restricted to users with the 'Admin' role.*

To access:
1.  Ensure you are in Admin Mode (Toggle available in **Settings** > **Development Tools**).
2.  Click **Admin Console** in the sidebar.

### Features
*   **Branding**: Change the community name and color theme instantly. Updates apply globally to your session.
*   **Access Control**: Manage guest policies and new member approvals.
*   **Content Moderation**: (Planned) Ability to remove inappropriate forum posts or marketplace listings.

---

## Mobile Usage
KithGrid is fully responsive.
*   **Sidebar**: On mobile, the sidebar collapses into a Menu button (top left).
*   **Navigation**: All features are accessible via touch on your phone or tablet.
